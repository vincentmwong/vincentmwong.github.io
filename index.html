I research how community dynamics influence individual beliefs, information sharing, and worldviews. My current research looks at the origins of social misperception, with projects on pluralistic ignorance and belief dynamics. I use a wide range of methods, including social media data analysis, dynamic network modeling, lab experiments, and surveys.
